# Lesson 8: Product Honesty And Interpretation

Today we fixed two product problems.

## Problem 1: Buttons That Did Not Work

Docking, ADMET, and Reports were visible in the sidebar.

But they did not actually work yet.

That is confusing for users.

So we changed them to show a clear "coming soon" message.

This is important in a company:

```text
Never pretend a feature works before it works.
```

## Problem 2: Numbers Without Meaning

A user may not understand:

```text
TPSA = 63.6
LogP = 1.31
Rotatable bonds = 2
```

So we added an interpretation panel.

It explains:

- size risk
- lipophilicity risk
- polarity risk
- flexibility risk
- hydrogen bonding risk
- shape profile

## Important Scientific Note

This is still a rule-based guide.

It is not a clinical prediction.

It helps students and researchers understand descriptor meaning before deeper ADMET or docking.
